The Province of Udine (It. Provincia di Udine, Friulian Provincie di Udin) is a province in the autonomous Friuli-Venezia Giulia region of Italy, bordering Austria and Slovenia. Its capital is the city of Udine.It has an area of 4,905&nbsp;km² and a total population of 518,840 (2001). It has a coastal length of 16&nbsp;km. There are 137 communes in the province.
