Apart from his versatility Regueiro is known for his quick pace and acceleration.Having spent five seasons with Racing Santander, Regueiro was transferred to Valencia in the summer of 2005 for €1.2 million.He has made occasional appearances for the Uruguay national team, including a friendly against England at Anfield, Liverpool, earlier this year.He obtained a Spanish passport in June 2006.
