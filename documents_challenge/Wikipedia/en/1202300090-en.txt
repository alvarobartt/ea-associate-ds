Bro Sant-Brieg/Pays de Saint-Brieuc, one of the nine traditional bishoprics of Brittany which used as administrative areas before the French Revolution, was named after Saint-Brieuc. GeographyThe town is located by the English Channel, in the Bay of Saint-Brieuc.
