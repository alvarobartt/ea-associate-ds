Costa del Azahar (Spanish for Orange Blossom Coast) or Costa dels Tarongers (Catalan for Orange tree Coast) is the name for the coast of the provinces Castellón and Valencia in Spain, from Alcanar to El Verger, Denia.Well known towns on the Costa del Azahar include Peñíscola, Benicàssim, Castellón de la Plana, Sagunt, Valencia, Cullera, Gandia, Denia, and Xàbia.This article incorporates text translated from the corresponding German Wikipedia article as of 3 September 2005.Azahar Category:Valencian Communityca:Costa de la Tarongina de:Costa del Azahar es:Costa del Azahar fr:Costa del Azahar nl:Costa del Azahar pl:Costa del Azahar sr:Коста дел Азар sv:Costa del Azahar
