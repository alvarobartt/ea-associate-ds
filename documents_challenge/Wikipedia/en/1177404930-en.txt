The Province of Turin (Piemontese: Provincia ëd Turin, It. Provincia di Torino) is a province in the Piedmont region of Italy. Its capital is the city of Turin.It has an area of 6,830 km², and a total population of 2,277,686 (31.12.2007). There are 315 comuni in the province (http://www.upinet.it/indicatore.asp?id_statistiche=6) &ndash; the most of any province in Italy.
