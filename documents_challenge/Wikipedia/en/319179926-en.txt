Ka, also Sekhem Ka Michael Rice, Who's Who in Ancient Egypt, Routledge 1999, p.86, was a predynastic pharaoh of Upper Egypt. He ruled over Abydos in the late 32nd or early 31st century BC and was buried at Umm el-Qa'ab. He most likely was the immediate successor to Iry-Hor and was succeeded by NarmerIan Shaw, The Oxford History of Ancient Egypt, p.71. He is the earliest known Egyptian king with a serekh, inscribed on a number of artifacts Toby A. H.
