 HNK Šibenik is a Croatian football club based in the city of Šibenik on the Croatian coast.The club was formed in 1932. In 2006 the club finished first in the Croatian Second League's southern division and was promoted into the Prva HNL.HNK Šibenik plays on Stadion Šubićevac (Šubićevac Stadium) which has a capacity of 8,000.Šibenik's fans are called Funcuti and are known for their hatred towards other two Dalmatian clubs, HNK Hajduk Split and NK Zadar.Current squadAs of September 4, 2008                            Notable players
