 leftarm1=FFFF00|body1=FFFF00|rightarm1=FFFF00|shorts1=000000|socks1=FFFF00| Club Deportivo Baskonia is a Spanish/Basque football club based in Basauri, Vizcaya. The club was founded in 1913 as CD Basconia and during its history has also been known as CD Vasconia . Although initially a separate club, since 1997 they have been affiliated to Athletic Bilbao acting as a feeder club. They currently play in the Tercera División - Group 4 and are ineligible for promotion unless Bilbao Athletic are also promoted.
