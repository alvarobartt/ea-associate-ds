He made 40 appearances with the reserve side before being promoted by Howard Kendall to the senior team in 1987, making his debut in a 0-1 defeat at Real Valladolid, on October 24. He subsequently made 172 La Liga appearances, and established himself as an outstanding man marker.Alkorta attracted the attention of Real Madrid, for whom he signed in 1993-94. At Real, he formed a notable partnership with Fernando Hierro. After 107 league appearances (scoring twice) and two titles, he returned to Athletic in 1997-98, helping the side finish second in his return.
