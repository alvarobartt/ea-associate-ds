 Cher (Occitan: Char) is an administrative department located in the centre of France. It is named after the Cher River.HistoryCher is one of the original 83 departments created during the French Revolution on March 4, 1790. It was created from part of the former province of Berry.GeographyThe department is part of the current administrative region of Centre (Val de Loire).
