 Montalegre (Portuguese pron. ) is a town and a municipality in Portugal, located in the Vila Real district in the north of the country. The municipality has a total area of 805,8 km², a total population of 12.150 inhabitants and is composed of 36 parishes.The present Mayor is Fernando José Rodrigues, elected by the Socialist Party.The municipal holiday is June 9.Coat of Arms and flagMontalegre's coat of arms features a red shield with a grey bricked castle. A blue label is in the bottom. The crown has four towers.
