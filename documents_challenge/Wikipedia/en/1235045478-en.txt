Ordino is one of the parishes of Andorra. It contains one of the main Andorran communities, El Serrat, as well as Ansalonga, Somàs, La Cortinada, Llorts, Segudet and Arans. It is home to the Sorteny National Park. It has a population of 3,066, as of 2005.
