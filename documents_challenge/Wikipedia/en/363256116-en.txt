Avolsheim is a commune in the Bas-Rhin department in Alsace in northeastern France.Historic Places:Eglise du Dompeter (The Church Of The House Of Peter)One of the oldest churches in Alsace. The current building dates from the 11th century and was built on the foundations of a Merovingian era church. Pope Leo IX,a native of Eguisheim, visited the newly rebuilt church in 1049.
