  The Gold-Striped Salamander, Golden-Striped Salamander, or Salamandra Rabilarga (Chioglossa lusitanica) is a species of salamander in the Salamandridae family. It is the only member of the genus Chioglossa. It is found in Portugal and Spain. Its natural habitats are temperate forests, rivers, inland karsts, caves, and subterranean habitats (other than caves).
