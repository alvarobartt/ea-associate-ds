Liliidae is a botanical name at the rank of subclass. Circumscription of the subclass will vary with the taxonomic system being used (there are many such systems); the only requirement being that it includes the family Liliaceae.Liliidae in Takhtajan systemThe Takhtajan system treats this as one of six subclasses within class Liliopsida (= monocotyledons).
