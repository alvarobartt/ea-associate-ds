He has won a number of honors including a nomination for an Academy Award for the title role in Cyrano de Bergerac and the Golden Globe award for Best Actor in Green Card.BiographyGérard Depardieu was born in central France in Châteauroux, Indre. He was one of the six children of Anne Jeanne Josèphe "la Liette" (née Marillier) and René Maxime Lionel "le Dédé" Depardieu, a metal worker and volunteer fireman.Gerard Depardieu Biography (1948-)http://genindre.org/arbpdf/depardieu.pdf&lt;/ref&gt; Depardieu spent more time in the street than in school and left school at 13. He left his home town of Châteauroux at the age of 16 for Paris. There he studied acting under Jean-Laurent Cochet. He achieved renown in 1986, playing the doomed hunchbacked farmer in the film Jean de Florette. More recently, he played Obelix in the three Astérix movies.This Europe: Confessions of Depardieu He has appeared in many films.In 1970 Depardieu married Élisabeth Guignot with whom he had two children, the actor Guillaume in 1971 and the actress Julie in 1973. In 1992, while separated from Élisabeth, he had a daughter Roxane Depardieu with the model Karine Sylla.
