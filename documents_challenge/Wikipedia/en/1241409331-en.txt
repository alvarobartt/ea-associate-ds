The Regions Morgan Keegan Championship is a men's ATP Tour event that has been held since 1976. Previously it has been known as the United States Indoor Championship, the Kroger St. Jude Championship and the Volvo Championships. In 2002, a women's WTA Tour event in Oklahoma City was purchased and moved to Memphis; thus a women's event was added, The Cellular South Cup which has been held in Memphis ever since. Both events are played on indoor hardcourts at the Racquet Club of Memphis each February.The primary sponsor of the Regions Morgan Keegan Championships is Regions Financial; while Cellular South sponsors the Cellular South Cup.
