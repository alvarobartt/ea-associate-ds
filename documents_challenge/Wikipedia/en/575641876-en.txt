 The Province of Viterbo () is a province in the Lazio region of Italy. Its capital is the city of Viterbo.It has an area of 3,612 km², and a total population of 299,830 (2005). There are 60 comuni (singular: comune) in the province, see Comuni of the Province of Viterbo.
