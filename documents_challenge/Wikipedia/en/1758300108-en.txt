Urals Federal District (; tr.: Uralsky federalny okrug) is one of the seven federal districts of Russia. It is the westernmost of the three Asian districts. Its population was&nbsp;12,373,926 in the 2002 Census, covering an area of 1,788,900&nbsp;km² (690,698.2&nbsp;mi²).
