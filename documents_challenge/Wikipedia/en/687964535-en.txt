 Hradec Králové Region () is an administrative unit () of the Czech Republic, located in the north-eastern part of its historical region of Bohemia. It is named after its capital Hradec Králové. Neighbours south with Pardubice Region, south-west with Central Bohemian Region and west with Liberec Region.
