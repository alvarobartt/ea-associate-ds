Josias Braun-Blanquet (3 August 1884 - 20 September 1980) was an influential phytosociologist and botanist. In his dissertation, supervised by Charles Flahault, he worked on the phytosociology of the southern Cévennes.His classification of vegetations along the lines of exclusively classifying vegetations according to floristic composition is what makes him one of the most influential botanists until today.Nicolson, M.
