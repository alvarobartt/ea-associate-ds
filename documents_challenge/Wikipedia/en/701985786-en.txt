 Jumièges is a commune of the Seine-Maritime département, in Normandy, France.Jumièges AbbeyIt is best known as the site of Jumièges Abbey, a typical Norman abbey of the Romanesque period, and the home of the pro-Norman chronicler William of Jumièges who wrote the Gesta Normannorum Ducum about 1070.
