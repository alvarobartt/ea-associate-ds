Harbour View W Connection(2 times champions)The CFU Club Championship is the annual international football competition held in the Caribbean Football Union/CFU region (Caribbean). The competition is open to the leading domestic club teams in the region. The winners of each national league qualify automatically. The winner advances to the CONCACAF Champions' Cup.
