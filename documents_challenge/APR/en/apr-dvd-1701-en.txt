this film is played by one of the biggest stars of the shaw brothers david chiang. in this film david chiang teaches the technique of praying mantis - a fighting technique was considered very hard. david chiang hired by the manchus must spy on a family posing for a teacher (scholar). but he falls in love with the girl he instructed and ended up marrying her. but he does not forget his mission. the fights are impressive, the scenery magnificent, yet a great movie directed by liu chia-liang.
