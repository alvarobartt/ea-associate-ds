Lefff (Lexique des Formes Fl´echies du Franc¸ais/French inflected form lexicon (Sagot et al., 2006)) is a large coverage (more than 110,000 lemmas) French morphological and syntactic lexicon (see table 1 for an example of a Lefff syntactic entry). In its latest public version, 2.2.1, Lefff contains 48 simple prepositions and 164 multiword prepositions.
