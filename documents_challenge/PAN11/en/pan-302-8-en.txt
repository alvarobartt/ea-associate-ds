 The patios in Cordoba and other cities of the province, are like those of Seville, surrounded
marble columns, pavements and fountains and flowers. In the smaller places are often be so rich
or so regular and architectural, but the flowers and plants are kept more love, with real care.
The lady, in the spring and the summer evenings, usually sewing or gathering in the courtyard, whose
walls are covered with a carpet of vegetables.
